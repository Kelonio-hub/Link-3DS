Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "Nintendo Files | La Presa" (hereinafter "we" or "the site") handles information from users visiting our website.

We do not permanently collect any personal data. The site does not require registration or mandatory entry of personal information.

2. YouTube Subscription

Access to the main content requires being subscribed to the YouTube channel "Kelonio 3DS".

Current status: Currently, subscription verification is done manually via a local timer. Clicking "Subscribe" opens YouTube in a new tab. We do not use the YouTube API or OAuth. We do not access or store any data from your Google/YouTube account.

In the future: We plan to implement the YouTube API with OAuth 2.0 authentication to automatically verify subscription. At that time:

  • We will request explicit permission to access only the public subscription status (scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly limited to subscription verification).
  • We will not request or have access to your password, emails, private videos, or any other personal data.
  • The access token will be used only in the user's browser (client-side) and will not be stored on our servers.
  • You can revoke permission at any time from your Google account.

All interaction with Google/YouTube will be subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • IP addresses
  • Account information (except, in the future, temporary subscription verification via OAuth)
  • Tracking or analytics cookies
  • Browsing or behavior data
  • Any sensitive personal data

4. External Links

The site contains links to external services (MediaFire, Mega, Amazon, Discord, etc.). Each has its own privacy policy, over which we have no control.

5. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy when new features are implemented (such as real verification with the YouTube API). The latest version will always be available on this page.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact us through our social media channels listed on the main site.
